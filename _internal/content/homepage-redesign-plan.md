# Homepage Redesign: Content Plan

Reference: [Cirrascale GPAR page](https://www.cirrascale.com/google-gpar)

## Current State

The homepage uses a generic enterprise AI message ("Transforming Business Through AI-Powered Solutions") with a 3D globe hero, three service cards (Amalgamy.ai, Sully's Sandbox, AI4JOBS), a feature slider, and hidden testimonial/blog sections. The positioning is broad and unfocused.

## Design Direction

The Cirrascale GPAR page works because it leads with a specific, high-value offering, names the partnership, and speaks directly to a defined audience. We should follow the same pattern: lead with Amalgamy.ai as the flagship platform, name the value proposition clearly, and target the buyer (enterprise, government, research).

---

## Hero Section

### Layout
- Dark background with gradient overlay (replace the globe/light theme)
- Centered or left-aligned headline with large, bold typography
- Subheadline + short body paragraph
- Two CTAs side by side
- Optional: subtle background visual (data center imagery, abstract infrastructure pattern)

### Copy

**Headline:**
Sovereign AI Infrastructure, Deployed at Scale

**Subheadline:**
The Amalgamy.ai Platform by ThisWay Global

**Body:**
Purpose-built AI and HPC infrastructure for enterprises, governments, and research institutions. From hardware orchestration to model deployment, Amalgamy.ai accelerates every stage of your AI journey.

**CTA 1:** Learn More (anchor to features section)
**CTA 2:** Contact Us (/contact)

### Brand Logos
Keep the existing partner logo carousel below the hero (NVIDIA, etc.), but consider refreshing to show only strategic partners relevant to AI infrastructure.
